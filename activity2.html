What is Soccer?

Introduction

Soccer is the world's most popular sport, played by over 250 million players in 200+ countries. The game is known for its simple rules and universal appeal.

Main Rules

  • Match duration: 90 minutes (two 45-minute halves)
  • Ball out of play results in throw-in or corner kick
  • Yellow/red card system for fouls
  • Offside rule prevents goal-hanging

Equipment Needed

  • Soccer ball
  • Goals/net
  • Shin guards
  • Cleats
  • Field markers
